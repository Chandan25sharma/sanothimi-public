# PART 4 — HOW WE USE THE DATA

## 4.1 Providing the SchoolSathi Service

We use the data entered by schools to:

We use institutional data to:

- Create and manage school accounts
- Process subscription payments
- Send subscription renewal reminders
- Provide customer support and training
- Communicate service updates and new features

## 4.3 System Security and Integrity

We use technical data to:

- Detect and prevent unauthorized access
- Monitor for unusual activity or potential data breaches
- Maintain audit trails for compliance and dispute resolution
- Debug technical issues and improve system stability

## 4.4 Product Improvement

We use anonymized and aggregated usage data to:

- Understand which features are most used
- Improve the user interface and experience
- Develop new features based on user needs
- Fix bugs and performance issues

We do NOT use individual student or staff data for product development or analytics.

## 4.5 Legal Compliance

We process data as required by:

- Nepal tax laws (for TDS, PF, VAT reporting)
- Nepal education regulations
- Court orders or lawful requests from government authorities
- Our own legal obligations as a registered Nepal company

# PART 6 — DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

## 6.1 Within SchoolSathi (Role-Based Access)

Data is strictly controlled by role:

- **Super Admin (Company staff):** Can access all school data only for technical support, bug fixing, or legal compliance. Access is logged.
- **School Admin:** Can access all data within their school only.
- **Accountant:** Can access fee records, salary data, and financial reports for their school only.
- **Teacher:** Can access student data for their assigned class only. Cannot see financial records.
- **Receptionist:** Can access student admission data and fee collection for their school only.
- **One School CANNOT see another school's data under any circumstances.**

## 6.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with carefully selected third parties who help us operate SchoolSathi:

| Service Provider | Purpose | Data Shared | Location |
|-----------------|---------|-------------|----------|
| MongoDB Atlas | Database hosting | All school data | Singapore (nearest to Nepal) |
| Cloudinary | Photo and document storage | Staff/student photos, documents | International CDN |
| Sparrow SMS / Aakash SMS | SMS delivery to parents | Phone numbers, message content | Nepal |
| Gmail SMTP / SendGrid | Email delivery | Email addresses, email content | International |
| Vercel | Web application hosting | No personal data | International |
| Railway.app | Backend server hosting | No personal data | International |

All third-party providers are bound by data processing agreements that require them to protect data with appropriate security measures.

## 6.3 Government and Legal Authorities

We may disclose data to:

- Nepal government authorities (IRD, police, courts) when required by valid legal process
- Regulatory bodies for compliance verification
- Law enforcement when required to prevent imminent harm

We will notify the affected School of any such request unless prohibited by law.

## 6.4 Business Transfer

If the Company is acquired, merged, or its assets are sold, student and school data may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to the same privacy protections. Schools will be notified before any such transfer occurs.

## 6.5 What We Will NEVER Do

- We will never sell personal data to any third party for marketing purposes
- We will never share student data with advertising networks
- We will never use student data to build advertising profiles
- We will never share staff salary or financial data with unauthorized parties
- We will never disclose student exam results to parties other than the school and authorized parents

# PART 7 — DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

## 7.1 Where Data is Stored

All SchoolSathi data is stored on MongoDB Atlas servers located in the Singapore region (closest available to Nepal with high availability). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

## 7.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

**Technical Measures:**
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit (HTTPS only)
- Bcrypt hashing for all passwords (never stored in plain text)
- httpOnly cookies for session management (prevents XSS attacks)
- CSRF protection on all forms
- SQL/NoSQL injection prevention
- Rate limiting on login attempts (max 5 attempts, then 15-minute lockout)
- Automatic session timeout after 8 hours of inactivity
- Role-based access control (RBAC) enforced at API level

**Organizational Measures:**
- Strict access controls — Company employees can only access school data for support purposes, logged and audited
- Regular security training for Company staff
- Confidentiality agreements for all employees and contractors
- Regular backup schedule (daily automated backups)
- Backup retention: 30 days rolling

**Monitoring:**
- 24/7 automated monitoring for unusual access patterns
- Audit logs for all data access and modifications
- Breach detection alerts

## 7.3 Data Retention

We retain data as follows:

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| Active school data | Duration of subscription | Service provision |
| Student academic records | 7 years after leaving school | Nepal education regulations |
| Financial records (receipts, invoices) | 7 years | Nepal tax law (IRD requirement) |
| Staff payroll records | 7 years | Nepal labour law |
| Audit logs | 3 years | Security and compliance |
| Technical logs (error logs, access logs) | 90 days | System maintenance |
| Backup data | 30 days rolling | Disaster recovery |

After the school's subscription expires:
- School has 90 days to download/export all their data
- After 90 days, data is permanently deleted from production systems
- Backup copies purged within 120 days of subscription end

## 7.4 Data Backups

- Full database backup: Daily at 2:00 AM NPT (Nepal Time)
- Backup location: Separate cloud storage from primary database
- Backup retention: 30 days
- Backup testing: Monthly restoration test

# PART 8 — RIGHTS OF DATA SUBJECTS

Under Nepal's Individual Privacy Act, 2018, individuals have the following rights:

## 8.1 Right to Access

Any individual (student, staff, parent) may request access to their personal data held in SchoolSathi. Requests should be made through the School Admin. The School Admin can generate data exports for individuals.

## 8.2 Right to Correction

Individuals may request correction of inaccurate personal data. This is handled through the School Admin who can edit records within SchoolSathi.

## 8.3 Right to Deletion

Individuals may request deletion of their personal data, subject to the retention requirements outlined in Section 7.3. Note that financial and academic records must be retained for the legally required periods even after a deletion request.

## 8.4 Right to Data Portability

Schools may export all their data from SchoolSathi in standard formats (PDF, Excel, JSON) at any time using the built-in export functions.

## 8.5 Right to Withdraw Consent

For processing based on consent (such as SMS notifications), schools and parents may withdraw consent at any time by adjusting settings within SchoolSathi or contacting us.

## 8.6 How to Exercise Rights

- **Through School Admin:** Most rights can be exercised by asking the School Admin to make changes within SchoolSathi.
- **Direct Request to Company:** For requests the School Admin cannot fulfill, contact us at privacy@sanothimi.com.np
- **Response Time:** We will respond to verified requests within 30 calendar days.
